About Whiteline Trading & Contracting

Whiteline Trading and Contracting W.L.L. is recognized as one of the leading suppliers of premium construction products in Qatar. Established in 2008, the company has built a strong reputation for its unwavering commitment to quality and exceptional customer service. Over the years, Whiteline has been a proud participant in many of Qatar's prestigious projects, delivering advanced solutions for plumbing and construction applications.

Our Mission

To forge innovative construction masterpieces that stand the test of time, employing environmentally conscious practices while delivering top-tier construction products and integrated services that cater to the evolving needs of Qatar's building and construction industry.

Our Vision

As Qatar's torchbearers of transformation, we envision a future where construction and climate control seamlessly coalesce, creating sustainable living spaces that enrich lives and safeguard the environment.
